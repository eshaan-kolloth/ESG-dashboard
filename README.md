## `💬` AI CHATBOT — SAMPLE INTERACTION

```
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  💡  WHAT IS AN ESG SCORE AND HOW IS IT CALCULATED?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  📖  EXPLANATION
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  An ESG score is a numerical measure of a company's
  exposure to Environmental, Social, and Governance risks
  and its management of those risks.

  📌  KEY POINTS
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  •  E (Environmental)  —  carbon emissions, energy use, waste
  •  S (Social)         —  labour practices, diversity, community
  •  G (Governance)     —  board structure, audits, transparency
  •  Scores typically range from 0–100; higher = better managed

  🧮  EXAMPLE  (Manufacturing Company · India)
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Environmental Score  →  62 / 100  (below sector avg: 70)
  Social Score         →  74 / 100  (above sector avg: 68)
  Governance Score     →  81 / 100  (top quartile)
  Composite ESG Score  →  72 / 100

  🛠  PRACTICAL TIPS
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  •  Start with BRSR (SEBI mandate for top 1000 companies)
  •  Carbon intensity is the single highest-weighted E metric
  •  Board independence directly lifts your G score

  ⚡  QUICK SUMMARY
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  ESG scores help investors, regulators, and stakeholders
  assess long-term sustainability risk in a single number.

  💬  YOU MIGHT ALSO ASK
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  →  How do I reduce Scope 3 emissions in my supply chain?
  →  What is BRSR and is it mandatory in India?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
```
